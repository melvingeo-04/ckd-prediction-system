About Kidneys

The Body's Natural Filter

Your kidneys filter about 200 litres of blood daily, removing waste and excess fluids. Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) occurs when kidneys are damaged and can't filter blood effectively — it develops silently over years, making early screening critical.
